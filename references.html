On this website, I believe I have done a few things well that I am proud of! Firstly, I am most proud of the website's design! I think that it is interesting and intriguing, and it ensures that the website is not boring! I feel like the design will actually improve the overall experience for the readers! Secondly, I am proud of the research I did. My goal was to convey the most important information to the readers, informing them, yet not being boring! I feel as if I was able to present all the information in a good manner. And finally, I am proud of enhancing my design with additional things using Fimga. I created the Cat Net's logo in Figma, as well as the summaries for each cat breed. I believe this elevated the overall level of the website, making it look and feel better and more professional.

The things that I would like to improve are the visual presentation of the cat breeds and the number of cat breeds available, as well as adding JavaScript. I honestly wish I had the time to add a few videos for each breed as well as more photos. I know that would have made the website even better. I also wish I added more breeds, again, if I had the time. Another thing would be JavaScript. I have no prior about JavaScript, but I still wanted to try and implement it into this website. Unfortunately, I did not have time for that as well.
