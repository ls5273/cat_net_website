Welcome to Cat Net! On this website, you will have the opportunity to find out about the world's most unique and interesting cat breeds! We have carefully chosen a few cat breeds with the most interesting appearance, history, and/or personality to present to you. On this website, you will be able to learn the key information about these breeds through our detailed descriptions of their appearance, personality, and history, along with a clear and organized visual summary of each breed. Together with all of the information available, there is an image gallery for each breed, filled with a great number of images that best present the breed's beauty!

The cat world is enormous, with more than 70 cat breeds in existence today! Each and every one with their own unique beauty and characteristics. Although all breeds are equally important and fascinating, some are more eye-chacking than others. Explore our website further and discover the beauty of the most breathtaking breeds that exist!
